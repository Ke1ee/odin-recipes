Classic Pepperoni Pizza

Description:

This Homemade Pepperoni Pizza has everything you want—a great crust, gooey cheese, and tons of pepperoni. The secret to great pepperoni flavor? Hide extra under the cheese!

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, grated
  • Ground black pepper
  • Flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 500°F.
  2. Mix seasonings and sauce together in a bowl.
  3. Roll out dough on lightly flowered surface.
  4. Add the topping of your choosing to the pizza dough.
  5. Cook for 6-8 minutes, then rotate the pizza half way so it cooks evenly. Cook for another 6-8 minutes or untill the dough is golden brown.
  6. Enjoy!
