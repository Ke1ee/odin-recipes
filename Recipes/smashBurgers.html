Smash Burgers

Description:

For the uninitiated amongst you, a Smash Burger does not involve hurling your brioche-bunned-bundle-of-joy at the nearest hard surface until well and truly flattened. Smash Burgers are not a thick, exquisitely grilled, medium-rare burger. They are not a lovingly crafted, perfectly symmetrical patty made with the most expensive cuts of beef and a heavily guarded spice mix. They’re minced beef and salt, smashed flat over a searingly hot flat-grill until you get a crispy, craggy-edged, asymmetric, cooked-through patty that’s juicy, caramelised, beefy and, frankly, the best burger you’re ever going eat.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Cheese of your choosing
  • Bacon
  • Toppings of your choosing
  • Brioche bun

Steps:

  1. Take handful of minced ground beef (you should end up with a puck)
  2. With a firm, flat tool, smash the burger down as hard as you like onto a very hot flat surface(aim for roughly 1-3cm in depth)
  3. Once flattened, heavily salt the top of the patty and leave to sizzle and cook for a couple of minutes. Under no circumstances should you move or disturb your patty
  4. Once you notice some color rising up the side of the patty, flip it, and salt again.
  5. Place your cheese of choice onto patty.
  6. Once cheese is melted. Place onto toasted bun and dress as you like
  7. Enjoy!
